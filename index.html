About Me

As a data scientist, I have a strong background in statistical analysis and machine learning. I am skilled in using a variety of programming languages, including Python and R, and have experience working with and wrangling large datasets.

One standout project of mine was the implementation of a machine learning algorithm to classify the city of a restaurant based on Yelp reviewer information. The algorithm, which was a Random Forest model, resulted in an accuracy of around 85%. I was able to highlight the importance of location for prospective and current business owners in Santa Barbara county, potentially changing the way that owners maintain their business.

Overall, I am a highly skilled data scientist and statistician with a history of meticulous and innovative work.
